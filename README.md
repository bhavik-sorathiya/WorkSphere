## 🎯 What is WorkSphere?

WorkSphere is built for teams who want more than just a simple to-do list. It serves as a unified digital headquarters where owners, managers, editors, and viewers can collaborate seamlessly. From organizing complex projects on drag-and-drop Kanban boards to discussing specific tasks in real-time chat, WorkSphere connects every part of your team's workflow in one beautiful interface.

## ✨ Core Features

*   **🏢 Organization Management**: Create a dedicated organization, invite members via email, and configure workspace-wide settings.
*   **📊 Dynamic Kanban Boards**: Visualize workflows with interactive, real-time task management customized for each project.
*   **💬 Real-Time Collaboration**: Instant chat messaging and task-specific comments allow your team to communicate without ever leaving the platform.
*   **📑 Document Storage**: Attach and manage documents, files, and rich-text notes directly within your workspace.
*   **🔍 Global Search**: Instantly find tasks, projects, comments, and members across your entire organization.
*   **🛡️ Enterprise-Grade Security**: Industry-leading authentication, strict role-based access control (RBAC), and detailed activity logging.
*   **🌗 Premium User Experience**: A stunning, highly responsive design featuring glassmorphism effects, fluid micro-animations, and full support for both Dark and Light modes.
